Mercedes GLC 200

Facts about Mercedes GLC 200

  • Under that bonnet is a new four-cylinder 2.0-litre turbo-petrol engine making 145kW of power and 320Nm of torque and shifting gears is a nine-speed automatic transmission.
  • The GLC 200 is also the only rear-wheel drive in the range, the rest being all-wheel drive.
  • Mercedes-Benz says you can expect a combined fuel economy of 7.8L/100km.
