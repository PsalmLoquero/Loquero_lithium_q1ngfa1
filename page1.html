Pagani Hueyra

The Pagani Huayra is a mid-engine sports car produced by Italian sports car manufacturer Pagani,

Facts about Pagani Huayra

  • The engine is a 6.0-litre twin-turbo V12 that has been cranked up from 730bhp to 778bhp.
  • The Huayra uses a seven-speed sequential gearbox and a single disc clutch
  • The BC version of the Huayra has a titanium quad exhaust which weighs just 2.9kg.
